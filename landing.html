Our Features and How They Benefit You

Events & Calendar

Discover & Connect: Explore a vibrant calendar of local events, from workshops and classes to community gatherings and festivals. Find activities that match your interests and connect with others who share your passions.

Stay Organized: Easily manage your schedule by adding events to your personalized calendar, receiving reminders, and staying up-to-date on the latest happenings in your community.

Create & Promote: Planning an event? Utilize ONE|Local's tools to create event listings, manage registrations, and promote your event to a wider audience within the community.

Resource & Skill Sharing

Share What You Have: Offer your skills, resources, and expertise to others in the community. Contribute to a culture of mutual support and collaboration.

Get What You Need: Request specific skills, resources, or assistance from your local network. Connect with individuals who can provide the support you need.

Build Community: Foster a sense of community and interconnectedness by participating in a shared economy.

Community Member Profiles

Connect with Your Neighbors: Explore profiles of fellow community members, discover shared interests, and build meaningful connections.

Showcase Your Skills: Highlight your unique talents, skills, and experiences. Let others know how you can contribute to the community.

Find Local Experts: Search for individuals with specific skills or expertise for projects or tasks.

Interactive Map

Explore Your Surroundings: Discover local businesses, services, events, and community resources through an interactive map.

Navigate with Ease: Find your way around your community and locate points of interest effortlessly.

Visualize Connections: See how you're connected to others and explore the network of resources available to you.
