Our Mission

ONE Local aims to empower communities by facilitating social coordination, resource sharing, skill development, and participatory governance. Our goal is to foster stronger local economies, enhance civic engagement, and create a resilient, interconnected network of communities.

Key Features

Connect & Collaborate

Build meaningful relationships and collaborate on projects that matter.

Resource Sharing

Share and access resources within your community to promote sustainability.

Skill Development

Offer your skills, learn from others, and grow together.

Participatory Governance

Engage in community decisions through transparent and democratic processes.

The Seven Foundational Pillars

  • Connection and Collaboration: Facilitating meaningful connections and collaborations within communities.
  • Resource Sharing and Sustainability: Promoting sustainable practices through resource sharing.
  • Knowledge and Skill Development: Fostering continuous learning and personal growth.
  • Community Governance and Decision-Making: Empowering participatory governance and decision-making.
  • Event Coordination and Participation: Organizing and participating in community events.
  • Personal and Community Wellness: Supporting well-being through resources and programs.
  • Communication and Information Sharing: Ensuring effective communication within the community.

How It Works

ONE Local operates on a tiered cooperative model that emphasizes local autonomy while promoting collaboration across broader geographical regions, referred to as basin regions. Users can connect with others, share resources, develop skills, participate in governance, and more, all through our intuitive platform.
