Value Proposition

ONE Local: From Isolated to Integrated. Find Your People. Build Your World.

Key Features

Community Networking: Connect with like-minded people, join groups, and build meaningful relationships.

Event Management: Create and manage events that bring people together and foster a vibrant local culture.

Project Collaboration: Organize projects, assign tasks, track progress, and work together to achieve shared goals.

Resource Sharing & Gift Economy: Share your skills and resources, request help when you need it, and participate in a thriving gift economy.

Marketplace: Discover and support local businesses that align with your values.

Collective Management & Governance: Manage your organization or community with tools for transparent decision-making, financial management, and member engagement.

Business Model

Cooperative Structure: ONE Local is a member-owned cooperative, aligning the incentives of members with the platform’s success.

Multiple Revenue Streams: We generate revenue through tiered memberships, transaction fees, premium features, and ethical investment opportunities.

Sustainable Growth: Our business model is designed to foster long-term sustainability and impact, creating a win-win for members, investors, and communities.
